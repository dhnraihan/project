About

Marufur Rahman

I'm Marufur Rahman-but most people call me Maruf. I’m a tech and finance enthusiast and a passionate problem solver with a strong interest in AI and scalable backend systems. I’m deeply curious about advanced algorithms and high-performance computing—tools I consider essential for tackling complex real-world problems.

I focus on turning innovative ideas into reality, always seeking opportunities where technology and business intersect to create meaningful impact. (Yes, my ESTP result doesn’t lie!).

I’m originally from Bhola, Bangladesh—not an opportunity-rich area, but a river-eroded island, where the unpredictable challenges of shifting rivers have shaped a mindset of resilience, curiosity, reasoning, and a touch of unpredictability. Growing up there instilled in me resilience, curiosity, and untamed energy.

I began exploring technology through competitive programming, which sparked my love for solving challenging problems and building intelligent systems. Since then, I have continually pushed myself to improve and have developed impactful projects, including AI-based initiatives and non-profit organizations.
